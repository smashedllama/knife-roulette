FAQ
How does this work?
Add at least two knives, then spin and lock one knife for the current week.

How do I add new knives later?
Use Add knives to open setup, add names, then press Save and start.

When can I spin again?
After you lock a pick, spinning is disabled until the next week starts.

What does Reset used do?
It clears the used list and unlocks the current week so you can start over.
